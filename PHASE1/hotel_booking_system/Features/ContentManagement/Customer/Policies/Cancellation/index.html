Cancellation Policy

Understand our cancellation and refund rules

1. Free Cancellation

Cancellations made up to 48 hours before the check-in date will receive a full refund to the original payment method.

2. Late Cancellation

Cancellations made within 48 hours of the scheduled check-in will result in a charge equivalent to one night’s stay. Any remaining nights will be refunded.

3. No Show

Guests who do not arrive on the check-in date without prior notice will be charged for the entire reservation amount, and no refund will be issued.

4. Date Modification

Guests may modify their booking dates subject to room availability. Changes may result in adjusted rates or additional charges based on the new dates.

5. Refund Processing

Refunds (if applicable) will be processed within 7–10 business days. The timeline may vary depending on the payment provider or bank policies.

6. Early Check-Out

In case of early check-out, guests will be charged for the next 24 hours from the time of departure, and the remaining stay will be refunded as per cancellation rules.

7. Group Bookings

For group bookings (5 rooms or more), a 7-day cancellation policy applies. Cancellations within 7 days of check-in may result in forfeiture of the advance payment.

8. Non-Refundable Rates

Special discounted bookings marked as Non-Refundable cannot be canceled or modified, and no refund will be provided under any circumstances.

9. Force Majeure

LuxuryStay will not be liable for cancellations or delays due to events beyond our control, including natural disasters, strikes, pandemics, or government restrictions.

10. Contact Us

For any queries regarding cancellations or refunds, please contact our support team at support@luxurystay.com.
