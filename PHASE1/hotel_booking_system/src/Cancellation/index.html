Cancellation Policy

Understand our cancellation and refund rules

1. Free Cancellation

Cancellations made up to 48 hours before the check-in date will receive a full refund.

2. Late Cancellation

Cancellations within 48 hours of check-in will incur one night’s stay charge.

3. No Show

Guests who do not show up without prior notice will be charged for the entire reservation.

4. Date Modification

Guests may postpone or change their booking dates subject to room availability and revised pricing.

5. Refund Processing

Refunds (if applicable) will be processed within 7–10 business days to the original payment method.
