Frequently Asked Questions

Click on a question to see the answer

What room types do you offer? +

We offer three categories: Deluxe Rooms for comfort, Executive Suites with added space and views, and Presidential Suites for a luxury experience with exclusive services.

Is early check-in or late check-out possible? +

Yes, both early check-in and late check-out are available based on room availability. Additional charges may apply during peak seasons.

Do rooms include complimentary breakfast? +

Yes, breakfast is complimentary for all room categories. Guests can enjoy a continental or buffet breakfast at our in-house restaurant.

Are pets allowed in rooms? +

For hygiene and guest comfort, pets are not allowed in rooms. However, we can suggest nearby pet boarding facilities if needed.

Do you have a swimming pool? +

Yes, we have a rooftop infinity pool offering breathtaking city views, open daily from 6 AM to 10 PM.

Is Wi-Fi free throughout the hotel? +

Absolutely. We provide complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi access across all rooms, suites, and public areas of the hotel.

Do you provide airport transfers? +

Yes, airport pick-up and drop-off services are available upon request. Please inform us at least 24 hours prior to arrival or departure.

Are there spa and fitness facilities? +

Yes, our hotel includes a luxury spa with wellness therapies and a state-of-the-art fitness center open 24/7 for guests.
