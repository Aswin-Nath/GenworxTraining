Privacy Policy

We value your trust and are committed to safeguarding your information

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, payment details, and booking preferences. This allows us to provide you with a seamless experience during reservations and stays.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process bookings, personalize your stay, improve our hospitality services, and provide you with relevant updates or promotional offers that may interest you.

3. Data Protection

We implement strict administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information. It may only be shared with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., payment gateways, transport providers) strictly for fulfilling your requests.

5. Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. You can disable cookies anytime in your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have full rights to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information stored with us. Please reach out to our privacy team to exercise your rights.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@luxurystay.com.
